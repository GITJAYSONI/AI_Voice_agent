What is ngrok?
Ngrok is a tool that creates a secure public URL for your local computer.
-To expose a local server (localhost) to the internet.
-To test webhooks (like WhatsApp API, Stripe, Razorpay, Twilio, etc.).
-To share your local project with anyone without deploying it.
-To test mobile apps or IoT devices with your local backend.

What is Twilio?
Twilio is a cloud communication platform that allows developers to add SMS, calls, WhatsApp messages, video, and email services into their applications.
-To send OTP SMS
-To send WhatsApp messages
-To make/receive phone calls
-To automate IVR / voice bots
-To send emails (using Twilio SendGrid)
-To handle webhooks from chat/call systems

What is TwiML?
TwiML is an XML-based language that tells Twilio what to do during a call or SMS.

-You use TwiML to give instructions like:
-What to say in a phone call
-What to send in an SMS
-Whether to record a call
-Whether to forward a call
-How to handle a response

What Is Deepgram?
-Deepgram is a voice-AI platform focused on speech recognition (speech-to-text), text-to-speech, and voice agent APIs. 
-It provides very accurate, low-latency transcription, even for real-time audio or noisy environments. 
-They support understanding/conversational intelligence: you can get insights like sentiment, topic detection, intent from speech.
